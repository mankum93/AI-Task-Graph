# Domain Profiles v08

This file summarizes the reusable domain profiles included with the v08 packet.

## Technical Book Authoring
Best for coding books, engineering books, technical manuals, and structured learning manuscripts.

Key lenses:
- learner progression and prerequisite discipline
- terminology stability and technical correctness
- code, visual, and prose alignment
- anti-templatization in batch chapter and diagram generation
- integration quality and whole-book coherence
- calibration-first scaling before bulk chapter production

## Book and Novel Writing
Best for fiction, memoir, literary work, and other long-form narrative branches where voice and continuity matter.

Key lenses:
- voice and register consistency
- POV discipline, timeline continuity, and pacing
- scene or chapter purpose and emotional movement
- anti-templatization in scenes and reflective passages
- integration without washing out distinctive emotional texture
- narrative-level coherence beyond local passage polish

## Coding and Software Implementation
Best for product engineering, app implementation, backend work, libraries, and tooling.

Key lenses:
- buildability and operational correctness
- interface stability and regression risk
- observability, migration impact, and rollback difficulty
- subjectivity-triggered calibration when API feel, UX quality, or architecture taste matter

Coding branches do **not** inherit the writing-specific QA doctrine by default unless the deliverable is heavily educational or narrative.

## Mathematics and Proof Work
Best for theorem work, derivations, olympiad-style reasoning, and formal or semi-formal proof tasks.

Key lenses:
- definition locking before proof use
- notation consistency and theorem dependency clarity
- case completeness and domain-condition discipline
- explicit reporting of proof gaps instead of pretending completion

## Problem Solving and General Analytical Work
Best for diagnostics, decision work, strategy, and analytical problem solving.

Key lenses:
- problem framing correctness
- alternative exploration and hidden-assumption detection
- counterexample resistance and edge-case handling
- explicit falsifiers and route comparisons
- DEEP_RESEARCH_AUDIT whenever current external facts matter

## Why the domain profiles matter
The general packet gives graph mechanics. The domain profiles add discipline-specific review lenses and evidence expectations so that the graph stays reusable without becoming vague.
